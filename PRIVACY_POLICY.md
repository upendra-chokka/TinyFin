# TinyFin Kids - Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 2026**

## Overview
TinyFin Kids is a children's educational app designed for kids aged 2-6. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with applicable children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

## Data Collection
- We do **NOT** collect any personal information from users or children
- We do **NOT** require account creation, login, or registration
- We do **NOT** track location, contacts, or device identifiers
- All coloring artwork, game progress, and activity data stays on the user's device only and is never transmitted to any server

## Advertising
- We use Google AdMob to display advertisements
- All ads are configured as **child-directed** with no personalized or behavioral targeting
- Ad content is filtered to be appropriate for children
- Users can remove all advertisements permanently with a one-time in-app purchase ($0.99)

## In-App Purchases
- One optional purchase is available: "Remove Ads" for $0.99 (one-time, non-recurring)
- Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play and tied to the user's Google Play account
- No payment or financial information is collected or stored by our app
- Purchase can be restored on new devices using the same Google Play account

## Third-Party Services
The app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob** — for displaying child-directed advertisements
- **Google Play Billing** — for processing the optional in-app purchase

These services have their own privacy policies:
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage
- All app data (coloring saves, custom worksheets, game scores) is stored locally on the device
- No data is uploaded to any cloud service or external server
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
This app is specifically designed for children and fully complies with COPPA:
- No personal data is collected from children of any age
- No behavioral or interest-based advertising
- No social features, chat, messaging, or user-to-user communication
- No links to external websites or apps (other than the in-app purchase flow managed by Google Play)
- No user accounts or profiles

## Parental Controls
- Parents can remove all advertisements by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option
- All content in the app is age-appropriate for children 2-6 years old
- No content requires or encourages sharing personal information

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
